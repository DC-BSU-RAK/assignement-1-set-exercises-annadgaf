Sinigang na Hipon

Sinigang na Hipon is a classic Filipino soup made with shrimp, vegetables, and a tangy tamarind-based broth. Itâ€™s warm, comforting, and perfect for rainy days!

Ingredients

  • 500g shrimp (with head and shell on, cleaned)
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 2 medium tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 liter water
  • 1 pack tamarind soup mix (or fresh tamarind)
  • 1 bunch kangkong (water spinach), trimmed
  • 1 cup radish, sliced
  • 1 cup string beans, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 2 pieces green chili peppers
  • Salt and fish sauce to taste

Method

  1. Boil water in a pot over medium heat.
  2. Add the sliced onion and tomatoes. Let them cook until soft.
  3. Stir in the tamarind soup mix (or mashed fresh tamarind) and let it dissolve.
  4. Add the radish and string beans. Cook for about 3 minutes.
  5. Add the shrimp and green chili peppers. Simmer for 5 minutes until the shrimp turns pink.
  6. Season with salt and fish sauce according to taste.
  7. Turn off the heat and add the kangkong leaves.
  8. Cover the pot and let the residual heat cook the greens.
  9. Serve hot with steamed rice and enjoy!
