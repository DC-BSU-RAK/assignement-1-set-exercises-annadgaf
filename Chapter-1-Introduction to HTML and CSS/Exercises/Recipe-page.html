(⁄ ⁄•⁄ω⁄•⁄ ⁄) Sinigang na Hipon Recipe (⁄ ⁄•⁄ω⁄•⁄ ⁄)

A comforting, tangy shrimp soup that's perfect for any weather! (≧◡≦)

✿ Ingredients ✿

  • 500g shrimp (hipon), cleaned
  • 1 liter water
  • 1 medium onion, quartered
  • 2 tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 radish, sliced
  • 5-6 pieces string beans
  • 1 cup kangkong leaves
  • 1 eggplant, sliced
  • 2-3 tablespoons tamarind paste
  • Fish sauce and salt to taste

✿ Method ✿

  1. In a pot, bring water to a boil. Add onions and tomatoes.
  2. Let it cook until the tomatoes are soft, then mash them to release flavor.
  3. Add tamarind paste, stir, and let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Put in the radish, eggplant, and string beans. Cook until tender.
  5. Add the shrimp and let it cook for about 3-5 minutes.
  6. Season with fish sauce and salt to taste.
  7. Turn off the heat and add kangkong leaves.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy! (ﾉ´ヮ`)ﾉ*: ･ﾟ
