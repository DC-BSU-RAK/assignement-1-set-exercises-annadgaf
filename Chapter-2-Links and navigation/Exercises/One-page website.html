Solo Leveling (◕‿◕✿)

Solo Leveling is a South Korean web novel that follows the story of Jinwoo Sung, a weak hunter who gains the power to level up infinitely after surviving a brutal double dungeon. Watch as he rises from the weakest to the strongest! (๑˃ᴗ˂)ﻭ
